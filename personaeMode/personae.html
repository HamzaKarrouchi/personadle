👥 Personae Mode — How to Play

🎯 Goal:
A mysterious Persona appears on screen...
Your mission? Guess who it belongs to!

🧠 How it works:
  • 🖼️ A Persona is displayed in full glory — observe its design, motifs, and overall style.
  • 👤 Enter the name of the character you believe is the rightful user of that Persona.
  • ✅ If you’re correct, the result is revealed and counted as a win!
💡 Tips:
  • 🎨 Persona designs often reflect their user's personality, backstory, or arcana.
  • 🔄 Some Personae are shared by multiple characters — context is everything!
  • 📖 Use your lore knowledge and visual intuition from Persona 3, 4, and 5 (and spin-offs).
❌ Give Up Mechanic:
  • 🔁 After 3 failed attempts, the Give Up button becomes available.
  • 🆘 Clicking it will reveal the correct character — but it will count as a loss.
🎮 Games Included:

Features Personae from Persona 3, Persona 4, Persona 5, Persona 5 The Phantom X and their respective spin-offs. Use filter buttons (P3 / P4 / P5 / P5X) to customize your experience.

🔍 Trust your insight, rely on your memory, and prove your Persona mastery!
