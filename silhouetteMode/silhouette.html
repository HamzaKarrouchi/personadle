Silhouette Mode Rules

🎯 Goal:
🕵️‍♂️ Guess the Persona character based on their black silhouette!

🕶️ Gameplay:
  • 🔍 You start with a zoomed-in black silhouette of a character.
  • ❌ Each wrong guess zooms the image out slightly, revealing more of the shadowy figure.
  • ✅ When you find the right answer, the full color image is revealed in all its glory 🎉.
🎮 Included games:
  • 🕹️ Characters can come from Persona 1, Persona 2: Innocent Sin, Persona 2: Eternal Punishment, Persona 3, Persona 4, Persona 5, and their spin-offs (P3P, P4G, P5R, P4D, P5S, etc.).
📉 Give Up option:
  • ⚠️ The Give Up button activates after 5 failed attempts.
🧠 Trust your memory, 👀 watch the shape, and 🔓 uncover the truth behind the shadow...
