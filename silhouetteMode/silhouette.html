🕶️ Silhouette Mode — How to Play

🎯 Goal:
A shadowy figure stands before you...
Can you identify the Persona character just from their black silhouette?

👤 How it works:
  • 🔍 The game starts with a heavily zoomed-in silhouette of a character.
  • ❌ Every wrong guess zooms the image out, revealing more of the outline.
  • ✅ Find the right name, and the character’s full portrait is revealed!
💡 Tips:
  • 🧍 Pay attention to the silhouette’s pose, hairstyle, weapon, or accessories.
  • 👓 Some characters have distinct stances or iconic features — spot them!
  • 🎮 Use your knowledge from Persona 3, 4, and 5 (plus spin-offs).
📉 Give Up Mechanic:
  • 🆘 The Give Up button unlocks after 3 failed attempts.
  • 🙈 You’ll see the answer — but it counts as a loss!
🎮 Games Included:

Characters come from Persona 1 , Persona 2 ( IS & EP ) , Persona 3, Persona 4, Persona 5 and Persona 5 The Phantom X including related spin-offs. Use the filter buttons to narrow it down.

🧠 Observe the shadows, trust your instincts, and become the ultimate silhouette detective!
