🔠 Emoji Mode Rules

🎯 Goal:
Guess which Persona character is being described by a sequence of emojis 🤔📱.

🧠 How it works:
  • 💬 You start with 1 emoji to describe the character.
  • ❌ Each wrong guess unlocks 1 more emoji.
  • ✅ Once you find the right character, the full emoji chain is revealed 🎉.
💡 Tips:
  • 🧩 Emojis may refer to: personality, arcana, story events, role, or weapon.
  • 📘 Example: 🗡️⚡😐 might describe a serious Persona user with a sword and electric powers.
  • 🔍 Try to guess based on context, not just literal emoji meanings!
🎮 Game flow:
  • ⌨️ Type a character’s name and choose from the suggestions.
  • 🔁 You have 8 tries max to guess the correct answer.
  • 🆘 You can press the Give Up button to reveal the answer (unlocked after 8 attempts).
🛡️ Spoiler protection:

🎮 Use the filter buttons (P1, P2, P3, P4, P5, P5X) to limit the characters drawn from specific games.
🙈 This helps avoid spoilers if you haven’t played every game yet!

✨ Let your intuition guide you... and read between the emojis 🧠📲
