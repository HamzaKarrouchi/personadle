Classic Mode Rules

🎯 Objective:
Guess the Persona character of the day by typing their full name or a known alias.

📊 How it works:
  • Each attempt fills a row with the character’s attributes:
    • Name — Full name of the character
    • Gender — E.g., Male, Female, Entity, Velvet, Shadow...
    • Age — "< 15", "15-20", "21-40", "40+", etc.
    • Persona User / Shadow — Can they summon a Persona? Are they a Shadow?
    • Persona / Shadow — The name of the Persona or alter ego
    • Arcana — Their Tarot Arcana (e.g. Fool, Empress, Magician…)
    • Opus — Games they appear in: P1, P2IS, P2EP, P3, P3FES, P3P, P4, P4G, P4AU, P4D, P5, P5R, P5S, P5T, PQ2
🎨 Feedback colors:
  • Green: Exact match
  • Yellow: Partially correct (e.g. one matching arcana or opus)
  • Red: Incorrect
  • ↑ / ↓: Age too young or too old compared to the answer
🧠 Hint system:
  • After 3 attempts: a quote from the target character may be revealed
  • After 8 attempts: you can reveal the answer using the Give Up button
🛡️ Spoiler protection:

Use the buttons in the top left to activate or deactivate the games whose characters can be selected.
This allows you to avoid being spoiled on characters from games you haven’t played yet.
For example, if you’ve only played Persona 4, disable P3 and P5 to avoid unexpected reveals.

Use your memory, your knowledge... and a bit of luck!
